Vegetable Sandwich

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • slices of bread
  • small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • Tomato, thinly sliced
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • Green chutney for spice

Steps

  1. Spread butter or mayo on each slice. Add green chutney if using.
  2. Place cucumber, tomato, onion, and potato slices on one slice. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Cover with another bread slice. Toast or grill if desired. Cut and serve!
