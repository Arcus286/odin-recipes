Parotha

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil or Ghee

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix 2 cups whole wheat flour and 1/2 tsp salt. Gradually add water and knead into a soft, smooth dough. Let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Divide the dough into equal balls. Roll one ball into a circle, spread a few drops of oil, fold it (into a triangle or square), and roll again gently.
  3. Heat a tawa or skillet on medium heat. Place the rolled paratha on it, cook one side for 30â€“40 seconds, flip, spread a little oil or ghee, flip again, and cook until golden brown spots appear. Repeat for all.
